A little bit about us

Fragment is a six man agency based in the heart of Bournemouth, focusing on interactive installations based in public spaces. We aim to engage with members of the public to create a memorable and enjoyable experiences for people of all ages. Our motto is Stay Snazzy, because we don't like to be too serious about things as we want everything to be fun and enjoyable for us and our clients.
